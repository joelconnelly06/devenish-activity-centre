About Devenish Activity Centre

Located in the heart of Northern Ireland’s natural beauty, Devenish Activity Centre is a community-focused destination offering inclusive outdoor and indoor experiences for all ages and abilities.

Our Mission

We aim to promote wellbeing, learning, and connection through engaging activities that inspire adventure and personal growth.

Our History

Founded in 2010, the centre has grown from a small local initiative into a thriving hub for recreation, education, and community events. We’re proud to serve schools, families, and organisations across the region.

Our Location

Devenish Activity Centre is nestled in the scenic countryside of Northern Ireland, easily accessible from major routes.
